HELLO! I'M MICHAL.

I'm a Computer Science and Math double major at Yale University and an undergraduate researcher. I'm obsessed with all things research, aiming to explore the frontiers of robotics and AI. My research focuses are in developing general purpose algorithms and robust robotic systems with internal representations more closely aligned with complex real-world environments. I have conducted research under the guidance of Brian Scassellati at the Yale Social Robotics Lab, Marynel Vázquez at the Interactive Machines Group, Dragomir Radev at the Language Information and Learning lab at Yale (LILY), and Nilanjan Chakraborty at the Interacting Robotic Systems Lab at Stony Brook University. I also love playing the piano, hiking mountains, and I'm starting to get my feet wet in Unity game development.
